Common Problems

My computer is really slow. Can you fix that?

Usually yes. Slow computers are typically caused by malware, too many startup programs, a full hard drive, failing hardware, or simply needing more RAM. We'll diagnose the cause and fix it — or tell you honestly if the machine is past its useful life and help you choose a replacement.

Can you remove a virus from my computer?

Yes. Virus, spyware, malware, and ransomware removal is one of our most common services. We'll remove the threat completely and give you practical advice on staying protected going forward.

Can you recover data from a crashed hard drive?

In many cases, yes. Success depends on the type and extent of the failure. We'll assess the drive and give you an honest picture of what's recoverable before you commit to anything.

My Wi-Fi keeps dropping. Can you help?

Absolutely. Intermittent Wi-Fi is one of the most common complaints we hear. The cause is usually a misconfigured router, interference from neighboring networks, outdated firmware, or hardware that needs replacing. We'll find it and fix it.

I think I got scammed online. What should I do?

Don't panic, but act quickly. If you gave anyone remote access to your computer, change your passwords immediately from a different device and call your bank if any financial information was involved. Then call us — we'll check your system for any software that may have been installed and help you secure your accounts.
